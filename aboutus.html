About Us

Our Story

Doulala is the next generation pregnancy platform, combining compassionate, expert doula support with tailored information to empower women throughout their pregnancy journeys and beyond. Doulala's founder, Folasade Runcie, was inspired to launch Doulala after witnessing her sister—a Harvard-trained emergency physician—struggle to find clear, reliable guidance and support during her own pregnancy. This experience highlighted how even the most knowledgeable and well-resourced women lack the necessary support and information during a critical time.

Our Vision

At Doulala, we leverage technology to bridge the gaps in traditional maternal care by providing:

  • On-demand access to trained doulas for emotional, mental, and practical support throughout pregnancy and postpartum
  • Personalized, easy-to-understand information tailored to each user's needs and preferences

By integrating these services, Doulala empowers women to make informed decisions, find community, and receive the continuous care they deserve, regardless of background or circumstance.
