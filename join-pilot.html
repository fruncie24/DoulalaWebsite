Join Our Pilot Program!

We're thrilled you're interested in joining us on this exciting journey. Please read the information below and click the sign-up button when you're ready to get started.

Doulala is a maternal health startup dedicated to providing personalized, continuous support throughout pregnancy by connecting women with expert virtual doula support. Our mission is to empower you with compassionate care and expert guidance, tailored to your unique motherhood journey.

We are launching our initial pilot program next month and would be honored to support you through this special time. The pilot offers flexible scheduling, personalized care, and access to certified doulas who are experts in prenatal and postpartum support. As a pilot participant, you will have unlimited access to small group "office hour" sessions led by our doulas, covering a range of important topics to help you prepare and thrive. For those seeking additional individualized care, one-on-one doula sessions can be booked at your convenience.

The best part? It's entirely virtual, making it easy for you to get support wherever and whenever works best for your schedule and lifestyle.

Sign up by October 17th to participate in our pilot

Pilot Details:

  • Who's eligible: Currently pregnant women and women less than 1 year postpartum
  • Pilot Start Date: October 20, 2025
  • Duration: 8 weeks
  • Cost: $50 to enroll in pilot and access group office hour sessions; $15 per one-on-one session booked with a doula
  • Location: Virtual — all sessions held via Google Meet
